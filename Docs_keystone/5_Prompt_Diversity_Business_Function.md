# **Diverse Prompts Tips**

---

Keystone Mortgage Partners — Task Categories — 6 categories and 19 subcategories. Each subcategory carries an authoring checklist (eligible personas, entities, systems, artifacts, linked scenarios), a worked example in the protagonist's voice, and inline definitions of every mortgage concept it touches.

Keystone tasks should reflect real mortgage brokerage work — loan origination, underwriting conditions, rate lock management, compliance reviews, closing coordination, borrower escalations, vendor disputes, pipeline tracking — not just "check the LOS and send an email." When you're stuck for a starting point, pick a subcategory and build from its worked example.

🎯 Use it to diversify. If all your tasks look the same, come back here. Each category exercises different systems (mortgage_los vs. QuickBooks vs. CRM vs. Stripe vs. filesystem), different entities (Keystone's own pipeline vs. borrower files vs. realtor partnerships vs. title company coordination), and different kinds of agent reasoning (TRID disclosure timing, DTI threshold calculations, rate lock extension decisions, income verification discrepancies, appraisal gap analysis, fair lending compliance, HMDA reporting accuracy). Your tasks should span that range over time, not cluster on one pattern.

⚡ Follow the patterns, not just the examples. The worked examples in each subcategory are illustrations, not templates to copy. Use them to understand what a rich, multi-action task looks like in this domain — then build your own from the authoring-checklist anchors and what you find in the live universe data.
